    Joseph Schwabauer   :   Full Stack Wizard

    Thanks for visiting my page. I am a website design, data and programming enthusiast. I built this website to show you a few of the things I can do. My background is, if nothing else, diverse. I've lived in the San Francisco Bay Area and North Carolina for the majority of my life but I was born in Michigan and lived in Texas when I was a kid. My first career straight out of high school was as a chef. After seven years of cooking in restaurants I decided to further my education. Five years later I found myself certified as an EMT with a B.A. in Writing and Research with an emphasis in Comparative Religion. Living in Northern California at the time, it was easy to catch the wine bug. I was absorbed in a second career for the next several years filling many roles in the wine world such as being a Wine Specialist for Whole Foods Market, a restaurant Sommelier and working with importers and distributors as a Sales Representative.

    It was in 2015 that I was exposed to computer programming for the first time. My wife had put everything down as a successful graphic designer and enrolled in a Web Design boot camp with the Iron Yard in Durham, NC. Through steady exposure I found a real interest and knack for all things algorithmic. I learned everything I could about programming, especially focusing on JavaScript and Python. With so many on-line resources I sunk myself into dozens of classes on Codecadmy, Coursera and several others. This period of intense study was fabulous and I craved a more formal foundation. So I decided to take a full load of Computer Programming courses at Durham Technical Community College to earn both a Software Development and a Database Programming certificate by the end of Spring 2017.

    My next step is to get a job applying the skills I've learned while exposing myself to exciting new challenges. In the meantime, I continue to develop my own projects and deepen my skills. If you think your company and I are a good fit, please feel free to reach out!
